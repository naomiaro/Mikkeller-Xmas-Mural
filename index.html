Limited Edition Oude Geuze (6.5%)

Limited Edition Oude Geuze

Acetic sting, an off-flavor to us, an acquired taste for others. Such as with this blend of 1,2 & 3 yr old lambic now, in the past, some champagne bottles would be given the name "Gout American" ("American Taste") when they were typified by a high amount of volatile acidity. With this in mind, we developed this limited edition both high in acidity while maintaining a delicate balance that is both complex and fruity. Pair this special blend with sharp cheeses or simply as an aperitif. 

 

Collaboration between Mikkeller & Brouwerij Boon

Milk Stout

Milk Stout (6%)

You are used to milk stouts? Im sure you havent tried one like this one. Dark chocolate on the nose and the palate, roasted malts and coffee that provide bitterness and smokiness but it balances out with the sweetness of the lactose. Overall a incredibly light and creamy stout with a smooth body thanks to the lactose. 

Warning! If you are lactose intolerant - don't drink this!

Ingredients: Water, malt, oats. lactose, hops and yeast.

Brewed by Mikkeller
